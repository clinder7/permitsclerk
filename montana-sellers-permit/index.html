Montana FAQ

Do I need a seller’s permit in Montana?

Generally no — Montana has no statewide sales tax, so there is no statewide seller’s permit. Montana has no general statewide sales tax, so no seller's permit is required (a few resort areas levy local taxes).

What if I sell to customers in other states?

If you sell online into states that do have sales tax, you may need seller’s permits there once you cross their economic nexus thresholds. We can file those applications for you.
