Frequently asked questions

A few things founders usually ask.

Who needs a seller’s permit?

Businesses selling or leasing taxable tangible personal property generally need one. Requirements vary by state and activity.

Is Permits Clerk a government agency?

No. Permits Clerk is a private filing assistance service. You may apply directly with the relevant government agency without paying our service fee.

How long does the application take?

Most customers complete the questionnaire in about 5 minutes. Government processing times vary.

What does the service fee include?

Guided intake, a review for common errors, filing assistance, support, and digital document delivery. Government fees, if any, are separate.
