Who needs a seller’s permit in Missouri?

Missouri requires registration for any individual or business selling taxable goods — even part-time or online-only sellers operating from home. The registration is issued by the Missouri Department of Revenue (via MyTax Missouri).

You need one if you…

  • Sell online to Missouri customers (Shopify, Amazon, Etsy, your own store)
  • Run a retail shop, boutique, or showroom
  • Sell wholesale to other businesses
  • Sell at pop-ups, fairs, or markets
  • Lease taxable equipment or goods

What you’ll need to apply

  • Legal business name and entity type
  • EIN (or SSN for sole proprietors)
  • Business address and contact details
  • What you plan to sell
  • About 5 minutes
Good to know: The Missouri state charge is free — the state charges nothing. The license itself is free, but Missouri requires a sales tax bond (roughly two to three times your estimated monthly tax) that is refunded after a good payment history.
